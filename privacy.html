Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2025

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy details how Braindump: Voice Memos & Notes, operated by BLACKSMITH SIA, handles your information.

Information Collection

We collect and process the following data:

  • Voice recordings (stored locally on your device)
  • Transcribed text (processed locally on your device)
  • Basic app usage statistics to improve our service

Data Storage and Security

All your data is:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Never shared with third parties without your explicit consent

Google Drive Integration

When using our backup feature:

  • We request minimal access to Google Drive solely for backup/restore functionality
  • Your data remains encrypted during the backup process
  • We cannot access your Google Drive data for any other purpose
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Export your data in common formats
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
